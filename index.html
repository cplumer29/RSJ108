Courtney Plumer's Guide To Composing a Thick Tweet

An Instruction Challenge for JOUR108

How to make an effective tweet for your audience ...

Assembly time: 5 minutes
Effectiveness rating: 100% effective

As journalists, we want to be informative and effective to our audience as much as possible. On social media, we have to be even better at communicating. With this guide, it will help you make an successful tweet for your audience. Follow these steps and you will flourish in composing tweets!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Phone or Laptop/Tablet
  • Photos
  • Video
  • Link to sources

  1. Go to Twitter.com.

    If you have a cell phone or tablet, you may use the app. If you are using a computer, enter Twitter.com into the address bar.

  2. Login or create an account if needed.

    Enter username and password in top right corner if you are using a computer. In the app, click 'Login In' located at the bottom. If you need to create an account, click sign up in the middle for the phone and computer.

  3. Click on the text box that says 'What's Happening'

    This is where you compose your tweet. Here you can add images, gifs and create polls. You can also add hashtags and tag people in your tweet.

  4. Create an effective tweet.

    To create the perfect thick tweet, include three hashtags, a link and a type of media. The link can be an online source, video, etc. The media can be a photo or a video. The media can help the reader understand what they are about to read before reading your tweet.

  5. Send the tweet.

    This tweet will appear on your homepage and profile page. If there is someone tagged or a hashtag in tweet, it will appear on the hashtag page and the tagged person or company page.
